Concerts

Concerts for the 2015-16 season

Welcome to OSM!

Concerts take on a new dimension when you're close to the action. For our 2015-2016 season, OSM will be performing all our concerts(*) in the intimacy and superb acoustics of the Ann and Jerry Moss Theater located in The Herb Alpert Educational Village at New Roads School. We hope you will enjoy the wonderful ambience, comfort, and viewing perspectives of this new 344-seat state-of-the-art venue. With acoustics designed by Yasuhisa Toyota (acoustician for Disney Hall), your live musical experience will be especially enhanced. For more information visit Moss Theater.
